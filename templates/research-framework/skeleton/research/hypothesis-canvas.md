## Assumptions to Validate

Assumptions are things the team takes as true without direct evidence. List them explicitly so research can confirm or challenge them.

| Assumption | Risk if Wrong | How to Test | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| Users have access to all three data sources | Solution design breaks | Ask in interviews | High |
| Manual reconciliation happens at least weekly | Problem urgency overstated | Survey + log analysis | High |
| Users would trust an automated solution | Adoption risk | Prototype testing | Medium |
